About Me

       I began my college career studying Spanish and education with the mindset of becoming an impactful teacher, sharing knowledge and meaningful content. After several amazing years of teaching high school Spanish across the country, new career options have been opened in the web development field, providing another outlet for me to grow and become passionate about.

       Implementing classroom technology in meaningful ways had always been a priority of mine, and this career was the logical next step to continue this trend. I first learned about web development from several friends and colleagues who had been employed as developers. Taking the necessary steps to secure a coding boot camp, research programming in my free time, and helping those around me with web design has been challenging but all the more rewarding. Seeing firsthand how coding can make a positive impact on those around me has given me the motivation to pursue this growing field of opportunity.
