About Me

Hi! My name is Benjamin Olsen, a full time teacher and native of snowy Wisconsin. In my spare time I enjoy wrestling bears, watching the Packers face the bears, and drinking bear I mean "beer." I graduated with an education and Spanish degree in 2017, and have been teaching high school Spanish ever since. I most recently moved to Washington this past June and have enjoyed my time here, even through the rainy weather. My friends and I have had opportunities to explore Mt. Rainier, downtown Seattle, and the surrounding area night life. There is a lot more to see, mainly restaurants, that I want to see and am excited to make this new place my home.

I do not have past experience coding but do have many friends and colleagues who have gotten into the profession. One close friend even took a similar boot camp and is now coding a website with his father. My hope is that this class will be able to open new career paths and strengthen my skills as a learner and professional.
